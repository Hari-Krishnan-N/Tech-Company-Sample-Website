Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

Our project process begins with an initial consultation to understand your requirements and goals. We then provide a proposal outlining scope, timeline, and costs. Once approved, we begin the discovery phase with detailed requirements gathering, followed by design, development, testing, and deployment. Throughout the process, we maintain transparent communication and regular progress updates.

We offer flexible pricing models depending on project size and requirements. For defined-scope projects, we typically use a fixed-price model with payments tied to milestones. For ongoing development or projects with evolving requirements, we often use a time-and-materials model. We require an initial deposit to begin work, with subsequent payments throughout the project lifecycle. All pricing and payment terms are clearly outlined in our proposals and contracts.

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope, complexity, and requirements. Small to medium-sized web applications typically take 2-4 months from concept to launch. Enterprise software or complex IoT systems may take 6-12 months. AI/ML projects often require 3-6 months for initial deployment, with ongoing refinement. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline tailored to your specific project needs and constraints.

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance services to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally after launch. We provide different tiers of support packages, from basic monitoring and bug fixes to full managed services with regular updates and enhancements. Our support team is available during business hours, with emergency support available 24/7 for critical issues on certain plans. We also offer training and documentation to help your team become self-sufficient.

We respect the confidentiality and intellectual property rights of our clients. For all projects, we sign comprehensive non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) before any detailed discussions. Unless explicitly agreed otherwise, all intellectual property developed during a project belongs to the client upon final payment. We maintain strict security protocols to protect your sensitive information and code. Our team members are all bound by confidentiality agreements as part of their employment contracts.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel who require it to perform their duties.
