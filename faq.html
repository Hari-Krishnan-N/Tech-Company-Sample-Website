General Questions

KRISH TECH is a technology solutions company that specializes in software development, IoT systems, AI/ML integration, and embedded solutions. We help businesses across various industries transform their operations and drive innovation through custom technology solutions tailored to their specific needs. Our team of experts combines technical expertise with industry knowledge to deliver high-quality, scalable solutions that solve complex business challenges.

KRISH TECH was founded in 2015 and has been operating for 10 years. Over the past decade, we've grown from a small software consultancy to a full-service technology solutions provider with expertise across multiple domains. During this time, we've successfully delivered hundreds of projects for clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies, building a reputation for technical excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.

Our headquarters is located in Silicon Valley, California, with additional offices in Boston, London, and Singapore. We also have a distributed team of professionals working remotely across various time zones, allowing us to provide 24/7 service and support to our global client base. Despite our geographical distribution, we maintain a cohesive company culture and collaborative work environment through robust communication tools and regular team meetings.

We serve a diverse range of industries, including:

  • Manufacturing and Industrial Automation
  • Healthcare and Medical Devices
  • Financial Services and FinTech
  • Smart Cities and Urban Infrastructure
  • Retail and E-commerce
  • Logistics and Supply Chain
  • Agriculture and AgriTech
  • Energy and Utilities

Our team has domain expertise in these industries, allowing us to understand the specific challenges and requirements of each sector and develop solutions that address their unique needs.

We specialize in developing custom solutions tailored to each client's specific needs and business challenges. While we leverage existing frameworks, libraries, and components to accelerate development and ensure quality, our solutions are designed and built specifically for each client's unique requirements rather than offering pre-packaged products.

Our approach involves a thorough understanding of your business objectives, challenges, and workflow before designing a solution that aligns with your goals. This ensures the technology we deliver provides maximum value and integrates seamlessly with your existing systems and processes.

Our development process follows an agile methodology with six key phases:

  1. Discovery & Analysis: Understanding your business requirements, challenges, and objectives through stakeholder interviews and research.
  2. Strategy & Planning: Creating a detailed solution design, architecture, and project roadmap with clear milestones and deliverables.

  5. Deployment: Carefully planned implementation with migration strategies, training, and go-live support.
  6. Support & Maintenance: Ongoing monitoring, updates, and improvements to ensure optimal performance and address evolving needs.

Throughout this process, we maintain transparent communication with regular progress updates and collaborative decision-making.

Billing & Projects

We offer flexible pricing models tailored to the nature and requirements of each project:

  • Fixed Price: A set price for projects with well-defined scope and requirements, providing budget certainty.
  • Time & Materials: Billing based on actual time spent and resources used, ideal for projects with evolving requirements or ongoing development.
  • Milestone-Based: Fixed payments tied to the completion of specific project milestones, combining budget predictability with flexibility.
  • Retainer: Monthly allocation of hours or services for ongoing support, maintenance, and incremental enhancements.
  • Hybrid Models: Customized pricing structures that combine elements of the above models to best fit your specific needs.

Project costs are determined based on factors such as scope, complexity, timeline, required expertise, and resource requirements. We provide detailed estimates and transparent pricing with no hidden costs. For precise pricing, we recommend scheduling a consultation to discuss your specific project requirements.

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope, complexity, and requirements. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Small Projects: Simple web applications or basic IoT solutions typically take 2-3 months from concept to deployment.
  • Medium Projects: More complex applications, enterprise software implementations, or multi-device IoT systems usually require 3-6 months.
  • Large Projects: Complex enterprise systems, advanced AI solutions, or comprehensive IoT ecosystems generally take 6-12 months or more.

Each project timeline includes phases for discovery, planning, development, testing, and deployment. We work with agile methodologies, delivering functional increments throughout the project rather than just at the end. This approach provides visibility into progress and allows for early feedback and adjustments.

During the discovery and planning phase, we'll provide a detailed project schedule with specific milestones and deliverables tailored to your project's unique requirements.

We offer both fixed price and time & materials pricing models, as well as hybrid approaches. The most suitable model depends on your project's nature and requirements:

  • Fixed Price: Best for projects with clearly defined scope, stable requirements, and predictable outcomes. This provides budget certainty but requires comprehensive upfront planning.
  • Time & Materials: Ideal for projects with evolving requirements, those requiring an agile approach, or when the full scope cannot be defined in advance. This provides maximum flexibility but with less predictable total costs.
  • Hybrid Approach: We can also structure projects with a fixed price for well-defined core functionality and time & materials for components that may evolve or require exploration.

For any pricing model, we ensure transparency with detailed documentation of deliverables, clear communication about any changes that might affect scope or budget, and regular progress reporting. Our goal is to find the pricing structure that best aligns with your project needs and budget constraints.

We accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate different client preferences:

  • Bank Transfers: Direct bank transfers (ACH, wire transfers) for domestic and international payments.
  • Credit Cards: Major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
  • Digital Payments: PayPal and other digital payment platforms.
  • Checks: For clients who prefer traditional payment methods.

For fixed price projects, we typically structure payments in milestones: an initial deposit to commence work, followed by payments tied to the completion of specific project phases. For time & materials projects, we generally invoice on a monthly basis with detailed time reports.

Payment terms and schedules are clearly outlined in our contracts and can be customized to align with your organization's procurement processes and payment cycles. We also offer enterprise billing options for larger clients with specific invoicing requirements.
