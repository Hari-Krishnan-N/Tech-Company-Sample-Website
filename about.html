Our Story

From Vision to Reality

KRISH TECH began with a simple yet ambitious vision – to make cutting-edge technology accessible and impactful for businesses of all sizes.

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate technologists with backgrounds in software engineering, data science, and hardware design, KRISH TECH started as a small consultancy focusing on custom software solutions for startups. As we grew, we expanded our expertise to include IoT systems, artificial intelligence, and embedded solutions.

Today, we're a full-service technology partner serving clients across industries – from healthcare and manufacturing to finance and smart cities. Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to excellence in everything we do.
